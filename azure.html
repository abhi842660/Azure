Welcome to Microsoft Azure

Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services, including servers, storage, databases, networking, software, and more, over the internet ("the cloud"). It offers faster innovation, flexible resources, and economies of scale.

Microsoft Azure is a leading cloud platform that provides a wide range of services for building, deploying, and managing applications through a global network of Microsoft-managed data centers. With Azure, organizations can enhance scalability, improve performance, and drive innovation.

Whether you're building a new application, migrating existing workloads, or leveraging advanced technologies like AI and machine learning, Azure has tools and services to help you succeed.
